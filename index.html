About Me



Sasindu Ruwaneka


I'm a



As a dedicated front-end developer and web designer, I’m passionate about bringing digital ideas to life through clean, intuitive designs and dynamic user experiences. I’m Sasindu Ruwaneka, a student at Rahula College Matara, where I began my journey in web development and design. What started as a creative curiosity grew into a serious pursuit, especially as I explored various web technologies and design tools. I quickly developed strong skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, enabling me to craft visually engaging and interactive websites. My preference for dark mode designs reflects my personal style, emphasizing both aesthetic appeal and user comfort.

In addition to web development, I also work as a freelancer, specializing in web development projects and graphic design for platforms like Discord. Through these experiences, I’ve become proficient in design tools such as Figma and Adobe XD, which allow me to create responsive and visually consistent layouts across devices. I enjoy developing user-friendly interfaces that balance elegance and functionality, focusing on accessibility and ensuring that my designs can cater to a wide audience. The process of collaborating with clients, understanding their unique visions, and translating those into effective websites has been incredibly rewarding, shaping my skills and adaptability.

Beyond my freelance work, I am also involved in managing the CricHub ,Web page where I stay up-to-date on world cricket news. This experience, along with my CricHub web project, has enhanced my abilities in both content management and creating user-centered digital experiences. Looking to the future, I am excited to continue expanding my skills as a front-end developer and web designer, committed to producing high-quality work that makes a meaningful impact.
